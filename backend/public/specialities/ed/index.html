Emergency Department Services – Medical Center Hospital Chattagram

The Emergency Department (ED) at Medical Center Hospital Chattagram provides 24/7 immediate care for life-threatening or acute illnesses and injuries. Our ED stabilizes patients, provides diagnostics, and handles traumas including heart attacks, stroke, and severe accidents. We are equipped to handle all types of medical emergencies with rapid assessment and treatment.

Emergency Services & Features

24/7 Emergency Care

Round-the-clock availability for trauma and urgent medical cases including heart attacks, stroke, and severe injuries.

Triage System

Initial assessment process that determines the urgency of a patient's condition, with the most critical cases treated first.

Trauma Center

Immediate response for major trauma including car accidents, falls, head injuries, and severe burns.

Cardiac Emergency

Rapid response team for acute myocardial infarction, severe chest pain, and cardiac arrest.

Stroke Care

Immediate treatment for stroke symptoms including sudden weakness, dizziness, confusion, and difficulty speaking.

Diagnostic Services

On-site imaging (X-rays, CT scans), laboratory services, and rapid diagnostic testing.

When to Use Emergency Services

Severe chest pain or pressure
Difficulty breathing or choking
Severe bleeding or major burns
Signs of stroke (sudden weakness, dizziness)
Unconsciousness or seizures
Major trauma (accidents, falls)
Severe allergic reactions
Poisoning or overdose
High fever with rash
Severe abdominal pain
Sudden severe headache
Emergency childbirth
